Digital Games

We sporadically release free games on Steam, Itch.io and other flash game sites.

Orestorm Factory is a BulletHell Roguelike where you build a factory of drills and processors to loot a cave, while dodging your way past enemies and your own deadly machinery along the way.

Can you keep the Mc'Chess kids out of danger and checkmate the opponent? The opponent will happily trade any piece in exchange for one of the Mc'Chess kids, so keep them safe, and most of all, have fun.

Deathcap Dungeon is a deckbuilder where your cards get stronger by not playing them.

Insecticide: Bugs vs Aliens dungeon is a tower defence game where you play as the enemy against an AI player that aims to emulate common tower defence strategies against you.

Board Games

Our board games are free to print and play on Itch.IO, use on tabletop simulator, or you can purchase a physical copy from the board game crafter once the builds are finalised.

Too Many Brews: The Proxy-Only Card Game is a pointed criticism of the limited accessibility of collectable card games.

Plant Dance is bag building game where everyone shares a single bag.

ChadWiz is a co-op deckbuilder where your cards change abilities over time

Goblin Alchemical is a deckbuilder where you don't shuffle your deck. On the surface it looks like a push your luck strategy game, but uses the chaos of a complex engine to suprise the player, rather than random chance.

Colorbot is a procedural puzzle game about instructing robots by giving colour-based directions. It explores the themes of the self and perspective by exploring how robots would observe the world around them.

Art

Other non-interact art peices we're working on can be found below.

Haxlore is a fantasy story built to explore the ethics of magical schools as a way to indoctrinate and weaponise aspiring mages.

Autonomous Decoupling is a science fiction story pondering the future of autonomous vehicles without sufficient legislation.

STOKS is black comedy exploring the ethics of running a business under capitalism. The initial spec scripts for six episodes has been written, we are currently looking for beta readers for the pilot.
